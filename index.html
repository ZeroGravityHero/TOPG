How to buy $TOPG

Download the Metamask browser extension or get the mobile app through your Appstore. Metamask will be used to buy $TOPG on the Solana network.

2. Buy SOL

SOL is the native token of the SOLANA network and required to buy $TOPG. You can buy SOL with your credit-card in the Phantom app.

3. Swap SOL for $TOPG

Visit raydium.io or jup.ag and connect your wallet on the top right corner. Add the contract address of $TOPG at the second field. Now chose the amount you want to buy and click on Swap.

4. That's it!

You have successfully invested in $TOPG and are eligible to use its ecosystem. Make sure you follow $TOPG on Twitter and join the Telegram community to stay up to date.
